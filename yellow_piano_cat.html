A Mentorship Program to Help Young People Reach Their Goals

This progam is designed to provide young people with the guidance and support they need to achieve their goals and build successful futures. Our experienced mentors will provide one-on-one advice and assistance tailored to the unique situations of each individual.

What We Offer

  • 1-on-1 mentoring and guidance
  • Help setting and achieving personal goals
  • Advocacy and support in finding resources
  • Advice on career paths and educational opportunities
  • Introduction to new skills and experiences

Eligibility

To be eligible for our program, participants must be between the ages of 14 and 21.
